How to play

Welcome to Flagle!

In this game, you will be shown a flag from around the world and be given 5 attempts at guessing which country or territory the flag represents.

You may enter in any combination of characters for your guess, and once you make your guess, each character will be highlighted to help you for your next attempt.

Grey means that character isn't in the word.

Yellow means that character is in the word but entered in wrong position.

Green means that character is both in the word and in the correct position.
